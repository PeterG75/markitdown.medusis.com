Mark It Down

Mark It Down lets you convert any rich text to Markdown, right in your browser.

Everything is local to the browser and the text you convert doesn't go to a central server.

The resulting conversion is real text that can be selected and copy-pasted in full.

(click to close)

Mark It Down is produced by Medusis and uses a number of open-source and non-open-source software:

  • Markdown was invented by John Gruber
  • the converter is written in XSLT and runs on Saxon-CE.
